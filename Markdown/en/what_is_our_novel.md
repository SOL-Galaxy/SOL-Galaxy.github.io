# Twin Stars Journey

> "When greed's poison seeps through every fiber of the consortium, when oppressed souls break their chains in desperation, that is when the old order crumbles and towers fall."

## Introduction

**Twin Stars Journey** is a science fiction story set in the **mid-21st century Twin Star Civilization Era**. The story unfolds across two habitable planets—the highly urbanized **β Star** and the resource-rich yet dangerous frontier planet **α Star**.

As the consortium's capitalist hegemony turns everyone into survival machines, and the indigenous Lingyi erupt in revenge after decades of oppression, protagonist Xing Su embarks on a journey to find her missing family, only to be drawn into a storm that will determine the fate of both planets.

This is a story about oppression and resistance, searching and loss, justice and its price.
